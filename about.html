About the application

This application is designed to help you track your progress if you’re interested in living with the philosophy called “The Nine Delights”. It means trying to experience at least three of the nine delights of the life each day.

The delights are:

Walking around - experiencing the pleasure of a good stroll
Fellowship - spending time with friends
Deliciousness - eating something tasty
Transcendence - the feeling of achieving the new level in something, doing something to the most and feeling good about it
Goofing - having a good laugh
Amelioration - working on betterment of something, a skill or deed
Coitus - acts of sexual nature that bring pleasure
Enthralment - the feeling of immense concentration on something, being incredibly engaged
Wildcard - anything you consider a delight that you feel doesn’t fit one of the above

For the benefit of you, the user, I created this application to help self-discovery.
On the main page you can tick off the delights you had this day and save them to the database.
Then, on the Statistics page you could see the progression of the delights in your life.
Enjoy!
